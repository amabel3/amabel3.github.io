About me

I am an NSF postdoctoral fellow at UT Austin working with David Ben-Zvi. I was previously a graduate student at MIT working with Michael Hopkins. I graduated in May 2022.

I mostly think about factorization algebras and their relationships with quantum field theory, elliptic genera, operads, and deformation quantization.
